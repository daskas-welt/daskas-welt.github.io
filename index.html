Malaga, Spain

Spent a few days in Malaga and loved it. The old town is full of tapas bars, the Alcazaba is worth the climb, and the Picasso Museum is a nice break from the heat. Great mix of beach and city.

Visited the Plaza de Toros in Seville. Whether or not you agree with bullfighting, the building itself is stunning and the history is fascinating. The whole city has this intense, beautiful energy.

Riomaggiore was my first stop in Cinque Terre and probably my favorite. Colorful houses crammed onto the cliffs, a tiny harbor, and narrow alleys that lead nowhere in the best way.

Hue Fortress, Vietnam

The Imperial City in Hue feels massive. You walk through gates, past pagodas and palaces, and suddenly understand how big the Nguyen Dynasty's world was. Easily one of the highlights of Vietnam.

Hoi An, Vietnam

Hoi An at night is something else. Yellow houses, paper lanterns everywhere, and the smell of food from every alley. I mostly walked around, ate too much, and watched the river.

Kedros, Donousa
Lesser Cyclades

Kedros Beach on Donousa has this shipwreck just sitting in the turquoise water. Crystal clear, no crowds, and a few free campers scattered around. Feels like the Cyclades at their best.

Stavros, Donousa
Main beach of Donousa

Stavros is the main beach on Donousa, right in front of the village. Shallow water, a few tavernas nearby, and everything you need for a lazy day. Good for families or just doing nothing.

Italida, Koufonisia
Lesser Cyclades

Italida on Koufonisia is the kind of beach you stay at all day. Turquoise water, soft sand, and somehow still relaxed even when people show up.

Ο Βασιλιάς Πίθηκος
Γου Τσενγκ Εν

Το κλασικό αυτό μυθιστόρημα γράφτηκε το 1570 από τον Γου Τσενγκ Εν και αφηγείται το περιπετειώδες οδοιπορικό του μοναχού Σαντζάνγκ και των τριών μαθητών του — του ατρόμητου Πίθηκου, του άπληστου Χοίρου και του λιγομίλητου Άμμου — από την Κίνα στην Ινδία τον 7ο αιώνα, στην εποχή της δυναστείας των Τανγκ. Σκοπός του ταξιδιού είναι να πάρουν τις βουδικές γραφές, ενώ στο δρόμο συναντούν δαίμονες, δράκους και τέρατα. Ο Πίθηκος, μαζί με τους συντρόφους του, χρησιμοποιεί χιλιάδες μαγικά τεχνάσματα για να προστατεύσει τον δάσκαλό του.
